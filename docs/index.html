Privacy Policy

Data Storage

YIMA: Period Calendar stores all data exclusively on your device using local storage (DataStore). No data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Collection

We do not collect, process, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No advertising or ad-related data collection
  • No crash reporting that transmits personal data
  • No access to contacts, photos, location, or other device data

Data You Enter

Period dates, daily records (flow intensity, mood, symptoms, notes), and app preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data is never shared with anyone.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings > Clear All Data. Uninstalling the app also removes all data permanently.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no data leaves the device, there is no risk of children's data being transmitted or stored externally.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in this page and in app updates. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely.
